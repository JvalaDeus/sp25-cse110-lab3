New Business

Allow teams to carry over their team page across seasons by updating the existing roster instead of creating a new page each year. Teams can simply add or remove players from the previous year’s roster, then publish it under a new name (e.g., Team Name 2025). This also enables easy access to historical stats by scrolling through past seasons.

When setting lines, allow users to organize players by position (e.g., handler, cutter) or, for mixed teams, by gender. This makes it easier to build balanced lines and quickly select players during games.
